# 1. RealEST Poll Intelligence System

*(40+ strategic questions for lead discovery)*

Your polls should **not just gather opinions** — they should collect **actionable market intelligence** that helps you:

• identify **demand hotspots**
• understand **budget ranges**
• map **supply sources**
• discover **investor intent**
• recruit **agents and partners**

Polls should be **segmented by persona**.

---

# Segment 1 — Buyers & Renters (12 questions)

Purpose: understand **housing demand**.

1. Are you currently looking for a property?

   * Yes now
   * Within 6 months
   * Within 1 year

2. What are you searching for?

   * Apartment
   * House
   * Land
   * Commercial property

3. Are you looking to:

   * Buy
   * Rent
   * Lease

4. Which city are you interested in?

5. Which area/neighborhood?

6. What is your price range?

7. How many bedrooms do you need?

8. What is most important to you?

   * Location
   * Price
   * Security
   * Property condition

9. Have you experienced fake property listings before?

10. How long have you been searching?

11. What frustrates you the most when searching for property?

12. Would you pay for **verified listings and property transparency**?

---

# Segment 2 — Property Owners / Landlords (8 questions)

Purpose: discover **supply sources**.

13. Do you own property you want to sell or rent?

14. What type of property do you own?

15. Which city is the property located in?

16. How do you currently advertise your property?

* Agent
* Online listing
* Word of mouth

17. What challenges do you face selling/renting property?

18. How long does it typically take to find a tenant/buyer?

19. Would you prefer a platform with **verified serious buyers**?

20. Are you open to listing your property on RealEST?

---

# Segment 3 — Real Estate Agents (10 questions)

Purpose: recruit **agents and agencies**.

21. Are you a licensed real estate agent?

22. Which city do you operate in?

23. How many property listings do you currently manage?

24. Where do you currently advertise properties?

25. What is your biggest challenge as an agent?

* fake leads
* fake clients
* fake listings

26. How many deals do you close per month?

27. Would you pay for **premium listing exposure**?

28. Would you benefit from **verified property leads**?

29. Would you like access to a **professional agent dashboard**?

30. Are you interested in becoming a **RealEST verified agent**?

---

# Segment 4 — Property Investors (6 questions)

Purpose: identify **investment demand**.

31. Are you interested in investing in property?

32. What type of investment interests you?

* rental property
* land banking
* commercial property
* development

33. Which cities are you interested in?

34. What is your investment range?

35. What information would help you invest confidently?

36. Would you use **market analytics and property insights**?

---

# Segment 5 — Developers & Agencies (5 questions)

Purpose: recruit **large listing suppliers**.

37. Are you a property developer or agency?

38. How many properties do you manage or develop annually?

39. Would you like early access to list projects on RealEST?

40. Would you benefit from **direct buyer demand insights**?

41. Are you interested in partnership opportunities?

---

# Segment 6 — Financial Institutions (3 questions)

Purpose: identify **future fintech integrations**.

42. Does your organization offer mortgage financing?

43. Would you be interested in connecting buyers with mortgage solutions?

44. Would your institution partner with a verified property platform?

---

# Poll Intelligence Outcomes

These questions help RealEST discover:

```text
Demand Locations
Budget Data
Agent Supply
Investor Interest
Mortgage Opportunities
```

This intelligence becomes **strategic product insight**.
